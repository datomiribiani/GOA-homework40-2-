Cars - General Information Cars are vehicles used for transportation. They usually have four wheels and run on roads. Most cars are powered by internal combustion engines that use gasoline or diesel fuel, although electric cars are becoming more popular. Cars are designed to carry a small number of people, usually up to five passengers. They come in different types, such as sedans, hatchbacks, SUVs, and trucks. Modern cars include many features for comfort, safety, and entertainment, such as air conditioning, airbags, GPS navigation, and music systems. Cars have played an important role in modern society by making travel faster and easier. However, they also cause pollution and traffic problems, which is why many countries are now promoting eco-friendly alternatives like electric and hybrid vehicles.
